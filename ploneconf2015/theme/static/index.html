"All-in-one" 2015 Plone Conference

Location

The conference will be held in Romania's vibrant capital, at one of Bucharest's more imposing landmarks, the Intercontinental.

Bucharest is an eclectic burst of metropolitan and intelectual ambition. Its many attractions, ecstatic night-life and immersive underground culture are within walking distance of the conference venue.
